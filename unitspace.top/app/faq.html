    FAQ

    Why Odo?

    Odo gives your users secure access to internal applications without the cost and
    hassel of managing VPNs. Odo expands secure access past traditional perimeter-
    based network security with the power to grant access to any application, to any user,
    from any device, while maintaining security hygiene.

    With Odo you can…

    • Differentiate between corporate and personal devices.
    • Limit sensitive data access to only corporate devices.
    • Limit remote access to specific applications without exposing the network.

    Odo is a SaaS platform that allows corporate IT organizations to provide secure
    connectivity to corporate applications and services for designated audiences.
    Luminate allows doing the above without exposing internal networks/data centers to
    risks associated with network access and provides unparalleled visibility and
    governance into activities that are performed by the accessing parties.
