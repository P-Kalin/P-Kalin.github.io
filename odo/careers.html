Odo is looking for an experienced DevOps Engineer to be responsible for build and
maintain our cloud infrastructure.

Our elite team is fast, innovative and flexible; with individual ownership. We expect
great things from our engineers and reward them with challenging projects, emerging
technologies and the chance to be part of a company that is about to change the very
foundation of remote network access.

The ideal candidate will share our passion and great pride in elegant solutions and will
join an engineering team that strongly believes in designing for scale, rigorous code
reviews, automated testing, and an iterative process to build high-quality next-
generation enterprise software.

Responsibilities

  • Build and maintain the company's entire cloud high scale production infrastructure.
  • Ownership of the company’s CI/CD pipelines
  • Implementation of logging and monitoring
  • Automation of data flows

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:

  • At least 3+ years experience as a DevOps/SRE, working with large scale cloud production
  • environments
  • Hands on experience in working on at least one of the leading cloud platforms: AWS, GCP,
  • Azure
  • Vast experience with CI/CD pipelines and automation
  • Experience in container orchestration - Kubernetes, Docker Swarm.
  • Proven ability in raising and maintaining logging & monitoring solution (ELK stack,
  • Datadog)
  • Solid understanding in networking
  • Working knowledge with SQL or NoSQL datastores and messaging systems
  • Experience in “Infrastructure as code”. Preferred: Ansible, Terraform
  • Strong background with scripting languages like Bash, Python in Linux environment
  • Knowledge of HashiCorp suite (Consul, Vault, Packer…) a plus
